Find My File

Smart Semantic File Search

A revolutionary Android application that combines powerful AI with uncompromising privacy, transforming how you search for files on your device.

Vision & Unique Selling Proposition

High Concept: "Find My File" is a smart, private, offline semantic search engine for Android that allows users to find files based on their content and context using natural language.

What Makes It Special: Combines a powerful ML-based indexing engine with advanced natural language processing that translates human language into precise, content-aware search queries—all while working completely offline.

The Intelligence Engine

Background Indexing

A lightweight background process continuously scans your folders, building a rich local search database with keywords, text, and metadata from every file.

Natural Language Search

Simply type what you're looking for in plain English: "show me photos from my trip last summer" or "find the report I downloaded last week."

AI Query Processing

Our compact 25MB on-device AI model acts as an "intent translator," converting your conversational queries into structured searches with high accuracy.
